Meet Moses Joya

Moses Joya founded his clothing brand in 2023 with the vision of blending urban style with timeless fashion. From day one, his brand has been about individuality, quality, and passion for fashion.

Alongside the clothing brand, Moses has been an accomplished DJ for many years, performing at top venues in Cape Town and beyond. As the resident DJ at RANDS, he brings energy and beats that keep the crowd moving. His music journey complements his fashion sense, creating a lifestyle brand that embodies creativity and culture.

Moses believes that fashion and music are powerful ways to express yourself. Through his brand and performances, he continues to inspire and connect with people who share his passion.
